DAYS 1-30

Listen, map, and stabilize

Phase 1
Phase goalUnderstand how the organization actually operates, build relationships, and take one or two low-risk things off your principal's plate.
Focus by week
Listening tour (aim for 12 to 20 conversations)

Use the same core questions with everyone so you can spot patterns.

  1. What is working well right now that we should protect?
  2. What is the most frustrating recurring problem you deal with?
  3. Where do decisions get stuck?
  4. What does your principal do that helps you most? What gets in your way?
  5. If you had my role, what is the first thing you would fix?
Track each conversation in your own doc: person, role, date, top theme, follow-up owed.

Build and prove

Phase 2
Phase goalPut your first system in place and show early, concrete value. Pick something visible enough to matter and small enough to finish.
Choose your first build (don't attempt all)
Weekly focus
Watch-outs
  • Don't reorganize things people rely on without explaining why.
  • Don't confuse activity with progress. One system that sticks beats five that people quietly ignore.
  • Keep your principal informed before the leadership team hears about a change, not after.

Phase 3
Phase goalMove from proving yourself to running a defined part of the operating system. Set up how the next quarter will work and where you fit.

Weekly focus
The 90-day readout (deliver to your principal)
  1. What I learned about how we operate.
  2. What I built and the evidence it is working.
  3. Where I now own the work, and where I still support.
  4. The two or three things I recommend we tackle next quarter.
  5. What I need from you to be more effective.
