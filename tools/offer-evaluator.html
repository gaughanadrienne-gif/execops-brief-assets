        How this is evaluated Components, not a composite grade

        Base is compared with an explicitly named observed band, median, or mean. Guaranteed cash and target cash are calculated separately. Equity receives an information-readiness status, not a value grade, and hypothetical private-option scenarios are kept outside cash compensation. No role, remote-work, or location multiplier is used.
